This is the first post on the **Dualmark** example blog.

Every page on this site has a markdown twin. To see it, append `.md` to any URL or send `Accept: text/markdown`.

## Why dual-mark?

AI search engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini) consume websites differently from humans. They want clean markdown without nav chrome, ads, or JavaScript.

Dualmark gives every page two representations — one for each audience.
